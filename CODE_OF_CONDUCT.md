# Code of Conduct

We commit to a collaborative, respectful, and harassment-free environment.  
All contributors are expected to uphold standards of professionalism, kindness, and inclusion.

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints.
- No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Maintain constructive, solution-oriented discussions.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality.

Report violations to: security@lumen-core.ai
