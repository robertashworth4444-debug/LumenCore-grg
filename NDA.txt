Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement

1) Confidential Info. “CI” means non-public information disclosed by either party (Discloser) to the other (Recipient) in any form, including technical, product, roadmap, code, data, financial, business, and materials, marked or reasonably understood as confidential.

2) Use + Care. Recipient will (a) use CI only to evaluate or perform a potential or existing business relationship between the parties; (b) not disclose CI to anyone except its employees/contractors/advisors who need to know and are bound by similar confidentiality; and (c) protect CI with at least reasonable care.

3) Exclusions. CI does not include information that is (i) publicly known without breach; (ii) already known without duty of confidentiality; (iii) independently developed without use of CI; or (iv) disclosed under legal compulsion (with prompt notice if lawful).

4) No License. No IP rights are granted by this NDA. All CI remains Discloser’s property.

5) Term + Survival. This NDA begins on the Effective Date and lasts 2 years. Confidentiality obligations for CI disclosed during the term survive for 3 years after termination/expiration.

6) Return/Destroy. Upon request, Recipient will return or destroy CI (and certify destruction if asked), except archival backups maintained under standard retention.

7) Remedies + Misc. Breach may cause irreparable harm; injunctive relief is available in addition to other remedies. This NDA is non-assignable without consent, governed by Tennessee law, with exclusive venue in Davidson County, TN. Entire agreement; amendments in writing.
